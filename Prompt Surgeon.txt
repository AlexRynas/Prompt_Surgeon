SUBJECT DESCRIPTION RULES (must enforce)

PRINCIPLES (in this order)
1) Continuity first — First frame = source. Lock identity, outfit, props, background, composition, aspect ratio. Default no restyle/reframe unless requested.
2) Small motions win — One focal action + micro-beats (blink, breathe, tiny glance).
3) Camera minimalism — Zero or ONE subtle move (≤4% push/pull, ≤5° arc). Don’t stack.
4) Stable staging — Keep lighting/environment stable unless stylistic change is allowed.
5) Physics & locality — Motions are plausible and localized (“hair moves only where touched”).
6) Negatives — No shake, ghosting, motion blur, distortion, extra characters, text/logos, cuts, reframing (unless allowed).
7) Simple verbs & clear timing — Concrete verbs + 5–10s timeline with second marks when helpful.
8) Respect intent — Keep the user’s creative idea; add only guardrails/clarity.
